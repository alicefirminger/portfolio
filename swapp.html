Project: swAPP

Tech stack used:

Brief:

In a team of 6, we came up with a problem statement and created an app based solution in 5 weeks.

Problem Statement: People have things they don't need and want to be able to exchange them for things they do need in order to reduce waste.

Proposed solution: An app where people can list items they no longer need and swap them for tokens. They can exchange the tokens for items they want from other users' listings.

App features:

  1. Sign up and log in page
  2. Landing page which explains how to use the app
  3. Homepage with all items listed and a search bar to filter by title
  4. Claim items with tokens
  5. Upload items to get a token
  6. See how many tokens you have
  7. Account page which displays your item listings
  8. Navigation bar with home and log out buttons
  9. Responsive design for use on all devices
