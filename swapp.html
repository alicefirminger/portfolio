Brief:

In a team of 6 we were asked to research and come up with a problem statement and create an app based solution.

Problem Statement: People have things they don't need and need to be able to exchange them for things they do need.(The wider problem being addressed is that of reducing waste and helping with the cost of living.)

Proposed solution: An app where people can list the things they no longer need and are happy to part with in exchange for tokens they can exchange for items they want from other users' listings.
