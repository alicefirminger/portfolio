Project: random recipe generator

Tech stack used:

React

Javascript

HTML

CSS

Brief:

In just one day, our team of 3 participated in a School of Code hackathon during week 6 of the course. Leveraging a recipe API, we created a random recipe generator as a solution to a user story.

App features:

  1. Click a button to reveal a new and exciting recipe.
  2. Recipe Image and Method Display: Experience the delight of seeing the recipe image and method displayed on your screen.
  3. Easy Recipe Closure: Close the recipe with a single click of a button.
  4. Unlimited Recipe Generation: Generate as many random recipes as you desire.

What I learned:

  • Using useEffect to fetch from APIs
  • Getting data from objects using dot notation
